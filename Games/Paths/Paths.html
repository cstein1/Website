Game of Paths

by Charlie Stein cstein1 `AT` trinity `DOT` edu


GOAL OF GAME: Your characters leave behind a blue trail which no character can cross.
You cannot continue to the next level until all red spaces have been painted
blue and your character has reached a respective goal.

Use arrow keys to navigate your character.
Use the 'r' key to skip this stage (your score will become 0).
Press the top left button to play with two characters! (WASDA for the second player)

Maps are randomly generated and thus have varied difficulty. Enjoy endless levels!
